🪲 Pods In Space - A NASA Martian Soil Experiment

A NASA Closed Ecosystem Experiment by Sarah J Trook.

The project aims to explore the feasibility of sustaining plant life in Martian soil conditions using a closed ecosystem. The experiment focuses on understanding how different plant species, isopods, and springtails adapt to the unique challenges posed by Martian soil, including its composition, nutrient availability, and environmental factors.
